**rss-util** is an RSS reader built with a specific philosophy: maximum utility, minimum friction.

## Design Philosophy

In a world of bloated feed readers with social features, algorithmic timelines, and subscription tiers, rss-util takes a different approach:

- **Focused** – Just the feeds, nothing else
- **Fast** – No unnecessary overhead or loading states
- **Portable** – Pure HTML/JavaScript, runs anywhere

## Why RSS Still Matters

RSS remains one of the most powerful tools for following content on your own terms. No algorithm decides what you see. No platform owns your feed list. rss-util embraces this philosophy.
