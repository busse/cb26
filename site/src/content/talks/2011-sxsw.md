Presented at SXSW Interactive 2011 with an analysis of **100,000 tweets** collected over 48 hours monitoring "coffee" during the conference.

## What We Built

A real-time system to monitor online communications for brands, demonstrating that social media data analysis could go far beyond simple word clouds.

## Key Topics

- **Twitter Search API usage** – How to collect and process large volumes of social data
- **Social CRM** – Understanding customer sentiment and behavior at scale
- **Data automation** – Building systems that process data in real-time
- **Pattern analysis beyond word clouds** – Moving from simple visualizations to actionable insights

## Why "Beyond Wordclouds"?

At the time, social media analytics often stopped at pretty visualizations. This talk pushed for more sophisticated analysis:

> Word clouds are great for presentations, but they don't tell you what to *do*. Real insight comes from understanding patterns, timing, and sentiment.
