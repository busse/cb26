Guest appearance on the Legacy Code Rocks podcast, hosted by M. Scott Ford and the team at Corgibytes. The show explores the world of modernizing existing software applications.

As CTO of APIvista, discussed what APIs are, what's critical to their success, and how organizations can find strategic success with digital initiatives. The conversation covered lessons learned from helping launch the first public API platform for a North American bank.
