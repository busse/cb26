A real-time system to monitor online communications for brands, demonstrating that social media data analysis could go far beyond simple word clouds. The talk analyzed **100,000 tweets** collected over 48 hours monitoring "coffee" during the conference.

## Key Topics

- **Twitter Search API** -- collecting and processing large volumes of social data
- **Social CRM** -- understanding customer sentiment and behavior at scale
- **Data automation** -- building systems that process data in real-time
- **Pattern analysis** -- moving from simple visualizations to actionable insights

Word clouds are great for presentations, but they don't tell you what to *do*. Real insight comes from understanding patterns, timing, and sentiment.

This was my first major conference talk. SXSW Interactive was the premier tech conference at the time, and this experience opened doors that led to speaking opportunities around the world.
