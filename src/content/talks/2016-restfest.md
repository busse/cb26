Speaker at RESTfest, the annual RESTful web services unconference held September 15--17, 2016 in Greenville, South Carolina. RESTfest follows an unconference format where every attendee participates as a speaker, fostering deep, practitioner-level conversations about REST, hypermedia, and web architecture.

The 2016 edition brought together a remarkable group of API community leaders including Mike Amundsen, Erik Wilde, Leonard Richardson, James Higginbotham, and Irakli Nadareishvili, among many others.
