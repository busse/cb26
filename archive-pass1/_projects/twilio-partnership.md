## Building the Twilio Partnership

During my time as **Chief Digital Officer at Terazo** (2018-2024), I helped build an extensive partnership with Twilio, establishing Terazo as a premier Twilio implementation partner.

## What We Built

We specialized in **Twilio Flex** implementations—helping enterprise clients modernize their contact centers with cloud-native solutions.

### Key Capabilities

- **Twilio Flex Contact Centers** – Full implementation and customization
- **Voice, SMS, and Conversations** – Omnichannel communication solutions
- **Enterprise Integration** – Connecting Twilio to legacy systems via APIs
- **COVID-19 Rapid Response** – Helping organizations go remote in days

## Speaking at Twilio Events

I represented Terazo at multiple Twilio events:

- **Twilio SIGNAL 2020** – "APIs all the Way Down: Enterprise Application Modernization & the Twilio-powered Contact Center"
- **Twilio SIGNAL 2021** – Moderated interactive roundtable on SIGNAL announcements
- **Twilio SKO 2024** – "Powering Twilio SMS with AI" video presentation

## Philosophy

> You don't have to replace everything at once. APIs provide the integration layer that allows you to modernize incrementally while delivering value immediately.

Our approach focused on meeting clients where they were—connecting modern Twilio solutions to existing enterprise systems, then gradually modernizing the entire stack.
