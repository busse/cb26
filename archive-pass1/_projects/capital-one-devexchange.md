## The First Public API Platform for a North American Bank

In 2015-2016, I led the launch of **Capital One DevExchange**—a groundbreaking initiative that made Capital One the first North American bank to offer public APIs to third-party developers.

## Why This Mattered

Banks had traditionally been closed ecosystems. DevExchange opened the door for developers to build innovative financial products on Capital One's platform, marking a shift in how financial services companies thought about technology and partnerships.

## My Role

As **Senior Manager of Developer Marketing & API Consumer Services**, I was responsible for:

- **Platform Launch** – Coordinating the technical, marketing, and business aspects of the launch
- **Developer Experience** – Ensuring the APIs were well-documented and easy to use
- **Community Building** – Founded API Craft RVA to engage the developer community
- **Developer Relations** – Building relationships with developers and gathering feedback

## Impact

- First public API platform for a North American bank
- Pioneered developer relations in financial services
- Established model for bank API programs
- Featured in [Capital One Tech on Medium](https://medium.com/capital-one-tech/behind-the-scenes-at-capital-one-devexchange-2b0702f2399b)
