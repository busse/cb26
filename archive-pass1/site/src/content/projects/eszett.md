**Eszett** works with artists who have a clear and principled stance: they reject AI as a tool for creating their art, but welcome it as a tool for the *business* of being an artist.

## The Philosophy

There's an important distinction between:
- **AI in art** – Using generative AI to create artwork (which many artists reject for valid reasons)
- **AI for artists** – Using AI to handle the business side: marketing, positioning, audience building, pricing strategy, and sales

Many talented artists struggle not because their work isn't good, but because they lack the time, skills, or inclination to market themselves effectively. Eszett bridges that gap.

## Services

- AI-powered marketing and positioning strategy
- Audience development and engagement
- Pricing and sales strategy
- Business operations for creative practices

## Why "Eszett"?

The ß (Eszett) is a unique character in German typography—a ligature that combines two letters into something singular and distinctive. Like the artists we work with, it's traditional yet irreplaceable.
