Led the launch of **Capital One DevExchange**—the first public API platform for a North American bank.

## Impact

This was a groundbreaking initiative in financial services:

- **Industry first** – No major North American bank had opened APIs to external developers
- **Developer community** – Built and nurtured a community of developers building on Capital One APIs
- **Platform strategy** – Defined the go-to-market strategy for the platform

## Key Contributions

- Developer marketing and evangelism
- API documentation and developer experience
- Community building and engagement
- Speaking at conferences to promote the platform
