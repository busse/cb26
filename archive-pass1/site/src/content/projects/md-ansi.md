A Python utility that converts Markdown text to BBS-style ANSI formatting. A nod to the bulletin board systems of the 80s and 90s.

## Use Cases

- Creating retro-styled terminal output
- BBS revival projects
- Adding nostalgic flair to CLI applications
- Text-based game development

Perfect for anyone who remembers the aesthetic of dial-up bulletin boards and wants to bring that visual style into modern projects.
