Explored API-first architectural patterns enabling Twilio-powered experiences (Flex, Autopilot) to integrate with legacy systems and deliver measurable business value, charting a path to fully cloud-native solutions.

This talk covered:

- **The API-first mindset** – Why APIs are the foundation for modern enterprise architecture
- **Legacy system integration** – Strategies for connecting Twilio to existing enterprise systems
- **Twilio Flex patterns** – Architectural approaches for building contact centers
- **Incremental modernization** – How to move from legacy to cloud-native without a complete rewrite
- **Real-world examples** – Case studies from enterprise implementations

The key insight: You don't have to replace everything at once. APIs provide the integration layer that allows you to modernize incrementally while delivering value immediately.
