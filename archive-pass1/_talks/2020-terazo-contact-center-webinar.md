Demonstrated how to transform in-office contact centers to fully virtual operations using Twilio Flex platform.

When COVID-19 forced contact centers to go remote overnight, organizations scrambled to find solutions. This webinar showed how Twilio Flex enabled:
- **Rapid deployment** – Stand up a fully functional contact center in under 30 days
- **Remote agent support** – Enable agents to work from anywhere with just a browser
- **Enterprise integration** – Connect to existing CRM, ticketing, and backend systems
- **Scalability** – Handle surge capacity without infrastructure investments

The session included a live demo of Twilio Flex and practical guidance for organizations facing the same challenges.
