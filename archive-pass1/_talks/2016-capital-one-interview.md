An in-depth interview explaining Capital One's motives for launching DevExchange—the first public API platform for a North American bank—and the opportunities it created for third-party developers.

## Why This Mattered

When Capital One launched DevExchange, it was a groundbreaking moment for the financial services industry. Banks had traditionally been closed ecosystems, but DevExchange opened the door for developers to build on Capital One's platform.

## Key Topics

- The strategic vision behind public APIs at a major bank
- How we approached developer experience (DX)
- Security and compliance considerations
- Opportunities for third-party developers
- What it took to launch an API platform in a regulated industry

This was a pivotal moment in my career and in the broader narrative of APIs in financial services.
