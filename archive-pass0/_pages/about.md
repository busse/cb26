Technology leader and speaker who has spent over two decades at the intersection of APIs, enterprise software, and digital transformation. Currently serving as **Chief AI Officer at SingleStone** and running my own consultancy **Eszett**, I help organizations modernize legacy systems and harness AI for competitive advantage.

My speaking career has taken me from Austin ([SXSW Interactive 2011](/talks/2011-sxsw/)) to Stockholm ([Nordic APIs Platform Summit 2018](/talks/2018-nordic-apis-stockholm/) keynote) to San Francisco ([Twilio SIGNAL](/talks/2020-twilio-signal/)). I'm known for making complex technical concepts accessible, including my signature **"APIs for Lemonade Stands"** workshop that teaches API-first design using a 1979 Apple II game.

## Career Highlights

- **Founded [API Craft RVA](https://www.meetup.com/API-Craft-RVA/)** – Richmond's API-focused developer community (288+ members)
- **Launched [Capital One DevExchange](https://medium.com/capital-one-tech/behind-the-scenes-at-capital-one-devexchange-2b0702f2399b)** – The first public API platform for a North American bank
- **Co-Founded APIvista** – API strategy consulting firm
- **Chief Digital Officer at Terazo** – Built an extensive partnership with Twilio, spoke at multiple SIGNAL conferences

## Expertise

I hold deep expertise in:

- **API Design & Strategy** – REST, OpenAPI, contract-first development, API governance
- **Twilio Ecosystem** – Flex, Voice, SMS, Conversations, Studio
- **Enterprise Application Modernization** – Domain-driven design, microservices, legacy system redesign
- **AI/ML Integration** – AI transformation strategy, conversational AI, NLP
- **Product Management** – Developer experience (DX), technical product strategy
